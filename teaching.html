Professor Chung has more than 20 years of teaching experience, both in classroom and online.

Courses

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

AI applications in business covering Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Planning for solving real-world business problems.

Machine Learning II

Second course building Big Data analytical skills using R, designed to advance data science proficiency beyond foundational methods.

Advanced Modeling Techniques (with Excel VBA)

Decision support systems design and programming with Excel VBA through hands-on coding projects and business modeling.

Careers for the Digital Age

One-credit professional development course taught with Timothy James from Google, preparing students for careers in the evolving digital landscape.

Information Systems and Analytics

MBA course exploring information systems strategy and analytics using Adventures of an IT Leader as the primary text.

Information Systems and Operations (BUS171)

First-year introductory course covering Excel, Access, website development, Google Analytics, and Tableau for business applications.

Digital Marketing in the Global Marketplace (BUS213)

Search engine optimization, Google AdWords campaign management, and web analytics for global digital marketing strategies.

Database Design for Environmental Sustainability

Database systems and design principles applied to sustainable decision-making and environmental data management.

Foundations of Business

Required first-year course providing a comprehensive introduction to core business concepts and practices.

Evidence-Based Management

MBA course applying systematic research studies to managerial decision-making and organizational strategy.

Evidence-Based Fraud and Forensics

Masters-level course at Carlow University focusing on fraud detection and forensic analysis using evidence-based research methods.
