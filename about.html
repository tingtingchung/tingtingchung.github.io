Research & Publications

Dr. Chung’s research appears in prestigious journals including the Journal of Association for Information Systems, Computers in Human Behavior, Communications of the ACM, Journal of Managerial Psychology, International Journal of Production Economics, Journal of Information & Knowledge Management, AIS Transactions on HCI, and Omega.

She has presented at major conferences including ICIS, ICAIF, Academy of Management, INFORMS, and SIGCSE.

Her research has been supported by the ACFE Research Institute, the Blockchain Lab of William & Mary, and the National Security Agency.

Teaching Approach

Dr. Chung uses creative pedagogical methods including sticky notes, chocolate, and her children’s book to teach AI and data analytics. She is the author of AI The Magic Box: An Activity Book on Artificial Intelligence. She has also created AI and data-inspired poetry, music, and co-designed data-inspired merchandise.
