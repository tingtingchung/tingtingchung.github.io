Media

BBC World News Feature

“Seeing Double”

BBC World News — August 6, 2012

Dr. Chung was featured in the BBC World News story covering the Twins Days Festival, where she participated in research on internet usage and security with twins. The research team included MBA students David Carroll and Amy Timo, Carlow graduate Jennifer Bourne, MBA candidate Maniak Mehta, JCC teacher Michelle Lu, and PhD candidate Chenjui Su. The study examined over 200 twin pairs and was funded by the Geibel Institute, Katz Graduate School of Business, and a $10,000 grant from the Institute for Fraud Prevention (IFP). The project was a collaboration with Dr. Dennis Galletta from the University of Pittsburgh.
