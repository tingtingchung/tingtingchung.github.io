About

Dr. Tingting (Rachel) Chung is a Clinical Associate Professor of Operations and Information Systems at the Mason School of Business, College of William & Mary. She holds two PhDs and a Master's degree from the University of Pittsburgh. Her teaching focuses on Artificial Intelligence and Data Analysis.

Born and raised in Taiwan, Dr. Chung is a native Mandarin Chinese speaker. She completed her Bachelor's degree in Psychology at National Taiwan University before earning a full scholarship to the University of Pittsburgh PhD program.
